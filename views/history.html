Technology Year First Implemented Brief Description
1G Analog 1983 Called Advanced Mobile Phone System. First used in Chicago. Range of frequencies between 824 megahertz and 894 MHz. Voice quality comparable to a wired telephone.
2G 1991 First generation of digital cellular technologies. Conversations digitally encrypted. Enabled services such as text messages, picture messages, and multimedia messages.
3G 2002 Speeds from 200kbps to a few megabits per second. First introduced mobile Internet access, fixed wireless Internet access, video calls, and mobile TV technologies.
4G 2009 Up to 100 megabits per second for high mobility communication. Up to 1 gigabit per second for low mobility communication. Frequencies ranging from 700 MHz to 2600 MHz.
