“Fastest”, “Most reliable”, “Better than ever”, we’ve all seen these claims in cellphone carrier’s commercials.

Promising “Blazing fast speeds” and “Amazing coverage”. Now more than ever, the internet of things follows us wherever we go.

With advances on streaming content quality, high-end mobile gaming devices, and smart vehicles we need a network infrastructure capable of sustaining all of this.

That’s where 5G comes into play. 5G is the fifth generation cellular network technology.

Every cellular network generation has technically been defined by its data transmission speeds and technological features with 4G being the latest prior to 5G.
